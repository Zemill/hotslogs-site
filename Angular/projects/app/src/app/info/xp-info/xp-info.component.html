This chart shows Team Experience gathered by each team throughout the length of a match.

  • The X Axis lists each Game Time minute
  • The Y Axis lists total experience, with blue bands for each Talent tier; dark blue for Level 10 and Level 20, and light blue for 4 / 7 / 13 / 16
  • You can Hover over any section of each bar to see exact values for that piece
  • You can Click to Toggle any category of Experience in the Legend on the right side of the graph
