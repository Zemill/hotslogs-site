Hero Roles are more specific roles for heroes. The normal roles (Tank, Bruiser, Healer, Support, Ranged Assassin, Melee Assassin) can be too generic for deciding Team Compositions. With the help of the community, I've assigned heroes to more specific Roles. These new Role categories and Hero assignments may change in the future.
